CTF Pony Fall by Gameboyer1719 (8/11/2019)

Use the left and right arrow keys to move the two ponies.

--> = Red moves right & Blue moves left

<-- = Red moves left & Blue moves right

Get to the trophy before time runs out and the trophy will be fake. Upon that, the level will start over once both ponies touch the fake one.
